How It Works

Our system processes review content, rating, sentiment, and helpful votes ratio to predict if a review is real or fabricated. We use advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to ensure accurate results.

What Inputs Are Needed?

  • Review Text: The content of the review.
  • Overall Rating: The star rating given by the reviewer (1-5).
  • Helpful Votes Ratio: The ratio of helpful votes to the total votes received for the review.

What Output Do We Provide?

After processing the inputs, our model provides a prediction on whether the review is genuine or fake. The output will be:

  • GENUINE - If the review is determined to be genuine and provides accurate, trustworthy information.
  • FAKE - If the review is determined to be fabricated or unreliable, often designed to mislead or manipulate.
